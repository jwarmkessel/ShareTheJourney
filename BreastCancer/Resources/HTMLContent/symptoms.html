Symptoms
Depending on treatment, there are a number of different symptoms experienced by breast cancer survivors. In this study, we are focusing on cognitive changes, mood changes, fatigue and the effect of sleep and exercise on these symptoms. There are many other symptoms including lymphedema (or swelling of a limb), neuropathy (that can cause numbness or pain) as well as many changes in quality of life.
